Incredible Apple Muffins

Current date and time will be displayed here.

On a cool Autumn evening or even a hot summer day, Apple muffins are always a hit and they make great snacks! Apple Muffins are so scrumptious and full of warm cinnamon and crisp apple bits and finished with a cinnamon sugar topping. My most special bakery style muffins recipe with amazing flavor and aroma, perfect to enjoy anytime.

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Blend the bananas, dates, and 1/2 cup of the apple juice concentrate in a blender or food processor until smooth.
  2. Then mix this with the rest of the apple juice concentrate, vanilla, and apples in one bowl.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients in another bowl.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir just until the dry ingredients are moistened.
  5. Pour into the muffin pan and bake at 350 degrees for about 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

List of Ingredients:

{DRY} Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups Almond flour
  • 1 cup Steel Cut Oats (I use Bob's Red Mill)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg

    • {WET} Ingredients

  • 1 - 12 oz can apple juice concentrate (no added sugar), thawed
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/2 cups chopped apples, peeled
  • 2 medium to large very ripe bananas, sliced
  • 4 large dates
